Memorabilia

Company: NYU Events Board
Roles: Promotions, Marketing, Logistics
Year: 2021
Memorabilia was the largest NYU Abu Dhabi event planned after the pandemic. Reaching about 500 students, the theme was based off of the famous television show Squid Game, only utilizing NYU Abu Dhabi's mascot--the falcon. For this event, I was leading not only promotions, but also logistics and the flow of the event.
