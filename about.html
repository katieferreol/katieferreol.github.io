Katie Ferreol is a multidisciplinary creative,
who specializes in graphic design, branding, and marketing.

Being young, enthusiastic and dynamic, Katie is a marketing and multimedia specialist with a passion for engagement and a proven track record of curating data-driven marketing campaigns. She utilizes her digital/design skills and expertise to help businesses reach their full potential. She is inspired by using design and marketing to craft elaborate stories using minute details, and is fascinated at how unnoticed they go by the unconscious eye. Being a Filipino designer who has been able to share her art both in the United Arab Emirates, Sweden, Philippines, and the United States, she has been able to take every experience and implement it into her work to create the best result for her clients.
